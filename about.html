About Me

My legal Chinese name is Li Jinyue as in 李今越. I was born in a small town in Hubei Province on August 27th, 2004 and grew up in Wuhan, China. I have been doing photography since I got my first camera in 2016. I have taken photos of many different places in the world with it ever since. In 2022, I also started doing freelance portrait photography for some clients.
